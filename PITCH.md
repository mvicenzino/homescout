- **Overwhelming decisions** - Hard to compare 10+ properties objectively
- **Financial uncertainty** - "Can we actually afford this?" remains unanswered
- **Information overload** - Too much data, not enough insights

### For Real Estate Agents:
- **Lost leads** - No system to track client preferences and urgency
- **Missed follow-ups** - Showings and feedback fall through the cracks
- **Client confusion** - Buyers forget which properties they liked and why
- **Manual processes** - Spreadsheets and notes don't scale
